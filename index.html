ICED COFFEE

ICED AMERICANO

An Iced Americano is made by diluting espresso with cold water and ice. It’s a simple, bold coffee drink with a clean, robust flavor—stronger than an iced latte but without the creaminess.

ICED MACCHIATO

An Iced Macchiato is made by pouring a shot of espresso over ice and then adding cold milk, often with a slight layer of foam on top.

ICED LATTE

An Iced Latte is a chilled coffee drink made with espresso, cold milk, and ice. It’s smooth, creamy, and has a balanced coffee flavor, with the milk softening the espresso’s intensity.
